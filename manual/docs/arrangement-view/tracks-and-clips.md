# Tracks & Clips

## Track Types

- **Audio tracks** — Record and play back audio
- **MIDI tracks** — Record and play back MIDI data, routed to virtual instruments
- **Aux tracks** — Bus/auxiliary tracks for grouping and effects sends
- **Master track** — Final stereo output

## Adding Tracks

- Press ++ctrl+t++ (++cmd+t++ on macOS) to add a new track
- Or use the **+** button in the arrangement toolbar

## Track Controls

Each track header shows:

- **Track name** — Click to rename
- **Track color** — Visual identification
- **Mute** (M) — Silence the track. Shortcut: select the track and press ++m++
- **Solo** (S) — Solo the track. Shortcut: select the track and press ++shift+s++
- **Record enable** (R) — Arm for recording

## Working with Clips

- **Move** — Click and drag a clip along the timeline or between tracks
- **Resize** — Drag the edges of a clip to trim it
- **Select** — Click a clip to select it; ++ctrl+a++ (++cmd+a++) to select all
- **Delete** — Select a clip and press ++delete++ or ++backspace++
- **Duplicate** — ++ctrl+d++ (++cmd+d++) to duplicate selected clips

## Drag and Drop

Drag audio files from your file browser directly onto a track to import them as clips.
